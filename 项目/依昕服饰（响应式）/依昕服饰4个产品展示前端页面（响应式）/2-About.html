Since it’s inception in 2006, Global Fashion House has become the local star in women’s fashion. Anna Zuo has established a sophisticated tone across all in house brands which include; All that Glitters, Ebby and I, White Closet, Paper Heart and Label of Love.


A lifetime’s exposure to the fashion industry, and mentoring from her parents encouraged Anna to pursue fashion and fortified her ambition to have her own label.


As these in house brands grow the team of designers are challenged to once again create ranges that are show stopping and appeal to the discerning customer. Global has established signature offerings such as luxe party wear, classic tailoring and everyday basics.


Our mission at Global is to be the no.1 fashion destination for women worldwide.
