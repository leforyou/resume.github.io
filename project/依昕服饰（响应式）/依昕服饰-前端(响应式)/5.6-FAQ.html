POPULAR QUESTIONS

Can I collaborate with Hello Molly on social media?

If you have a wicked social media presence, Instagram page or are just interested in collaborating with the Hello Molly brand we’d LOVE to hear from you! Please contact our Creative Team at creative@hellomolly.com for all PR, Marketing and collaboration enquiries.

Do you have a store I can visit?

Hello Molly is exclusively online! This means we can serve customers all across the world but unfortunately we don't have a physical location where you can come and try on garments before buying. Our Rosebery office is closed to the public but we do allow for pick-ups on existing orders that have not yet been sent out. Simply call 02 9017 0300 if you wish to organise a pick-up.

How can I access my store credit?

Store credit is issued to accounts. If your Hello Molly account has credit on it, make sure you are logged when you check out. Under ‘Payment Method’ on the checkout page, you will have the option to apply your credit to the order! If you make a return and don’t have an account with us, we will create one for you using the email address associated with your original order. Also please note, accounts are not transferable between our Australian and US sites.

How long will it take for me to receive my order?

All orders received before 2pm local Sydney time on a business day are shipped out that same day. Otherwise they’re sent out the next business day. We ship everything express so if you are within an Australian Metro Area (check here) that means next business day delivery! Delivery to the USA typically takes 3-5 business days from the dispatch date. For all other countries, it could take up to 7 business days.

Please refer to our Shipping Page for full details.

I didn’t receive an order or shipping confirmation. What should I do?

We’re sorry to hear that. It’s possible the email has gone to your junk folder or maybe we have an incorrect email address for you. If this happens, please contact us straight away so we can verify whether the order has been placed and resend your confirmation. Australian customers, please call 1300 436 956. Overseas customers can reach us on 00 612 9017 0300.

I want to call you. What are your working hours?

We’re in the office from 9.30am-5pm local Sydney time Monday-Friday (excluding public holidays). Australian customers, please call 1300 436 956. Overseas customers can reach us on 00 612 9017 0300.

Where are you located?

We’re based in sunny Sydney, Australia!
